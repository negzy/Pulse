How to use

  1. Open the Pulse dashboard in your browser (URL above), log in, go to Extension in the menu, and copy your token here.
  2. Go to skool.com and open one of your groups. Click the Skool Pulse icon → side panel opens.
  3. Click Sync to Pulse in the panel to save today’s numbers to your dashboard.
